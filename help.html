Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?
Simply browse our services, select the one you need, choose a professional, pick a time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation with all the details.
Are all professionals verified?
Yes, all professionals undergo a thorough background verification process including identity checks, skill assessments, and reference verification before joining our platform.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and digital wallets. You can also pay cash to the professional after service completion.
Can I reschedule my booking?
Yes, you can reschedule your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled time without any charges. For last-minute changes, cancellation fees may apply.
What if I'm not satisfied with the service?
We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the service, contact our support team within 24 hours and we'll arrange for a re-service or provide a refund.
